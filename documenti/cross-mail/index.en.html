Description:

The scripts in this package let you copy your mail between Evolution, KMail e Mozilla.

They preserve folders hierarchy, this is why they are cool! :-)


Installation:

BACK UP YOUR MAIL FOLDERS!

Download tar.gz from Link on top.

Unpack it on your HOME directory typing:



Usage:

OPERATION

COMMAND

NOTES

EVOLUTION

to

KMAIL:

$ ./ev2kmail


KMAIL

to

EVOLUTION:

$ ./kmail2ev

Open Evolution and close it before running the script. (This is needed becouse evolution creates an evolution folder in your HOME)

EVOLUTION

to

MOZILLA:

$ ./ev2moz <moz mail dir>

<moz mail dir>: mozilla Mail directory

( e.g.: ~/.mozilla/<user>/<code>/Mail )

MOZILLA

to

EVOLUTION:

$ ./moz2ev <moz mail dir>

<moz mail dir>: mozilla Mail directory

( e.g.: ~/.mozilla/<user>/<code>/Mail )
